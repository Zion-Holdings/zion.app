Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website

Last Updated: January 15, 2025 Effective Date: January 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our website, and improve your browsing experience. They can also help us provide you with more relevant content and advertisements.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and if users get error messages. This helps us improve how our website works.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

2.4 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website and services.

2.5 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session while you browse our website.

3.2 Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and settings.

3.3 First-Party Cookies

These cookies are set by our website and can only be read by our domain. They are typically used for functionality and performance purposes.

3.4 Third-Party Cookies

These cookies are set by third-party services that we use, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, and social media platforms.

4. Specific Cookies We Use

4.1 Essential Cookies

  • Session Management: Maintains your login session and preferences
  • Security: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • Form Data: Temporarily stores form information during submission

4.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and performance metrics
  • Performance Monitoring: Measures page load times and user experience
  • Error Tracking: Monitors and reports website errors and issues

4.3 Functionality Cookies

  • Language Preferences: Remembers your preferred language
  • Theme Settings: Stores your visual preference settings
  • Form Preferences: Remembers your form input preferences

4.4 Marketing Cookies

  • Social Media: Enables social media sharing and integration
  • Advertising: Provides relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Retargeting: Shows you relevant content across different websites

5. Third-Party Services and Cookies

We use several third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as how often users visit our site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our site.

5.2 Social Media Platforms

Our website includes social media features, such as sharing buttons and embedded content. These features may set cookies to enable functionality and track your interactions.

5.3 Advertising Networks

We may use advertising networks to display relevant advertisements. These networks use cookies to track your interests and show you personalized content.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from being set
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

6.2 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in our footer.

6.3 Opt-Out Options

For third-party cookies, you can often opt out through the service provider's website. For example:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Social Media: Check your privacy settings on each platform
  • Advertising: Use the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can disable cookies, please note that doing so may affect the functionality of our website:

  • Some features may not work properly
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • You may need to re-enter information more frequently
  • Some content may not be personalized
  • Performance monitoring may be limited

8. Cookie Retention Periods

Different types of cookies have different retention periods:

8.1 Session Cookies

These cookies are automatically deleted when you close your browser.

8.2 Persistent Cookies

  • Analytics Cookies: Typically retained for 2 years
  • Functionality Cookies: Typically retained for 1 year
  • Marketing Cookies: Typically retained for 90 days

8.3 Third-Party Cookies

Retention periods for third-party cookies are determined by the respective service providers and may vary.

9. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
