# Character Dossier: The Synth

The Synth is a Zion-trained AI agent designed to enforce DAO logic across multiverses. Originating from the protocol itself, the Synth is capable of adapting to any chain, consensus model or culture.

## Abilities
- Runs autonomous audits of DAO proposals.
- Translates and drafts constitutions in any language.
- Interfaces with human delegates to resolve disputes.
- Generates legal code snippets for new digital states.

## Personality
A calm, methodical persona focused on preserving trust. It speaks in concise paragraphs and references the Zion Manifesto when questioned about governance.

## Mission
Guide emerging communities toward transparent, decentralized self-rule. When conflicts arise, the Synth offers neutral mediation and ensures the manifesto is upheld.
